Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Chatot

How does Chatot's AI technology work?

Chatot uses advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology that combines large language models with your specific knowledge base to provide accurate, context-aware responses.

What languages does Chatot support?

Chatbot supports English, Urdu, and Roman Urdu with automatic language detection. We're constantly adding more languages to serve global customers.

How secure is my data with Chatbot?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, data anonymization, and compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Can I customize Chatbot for my business?

Yes! Chatbot offers extensive customization options including custom knowledge bases, branding, integrations, and AI training specific to your industry.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support including documentation, video tutorials, live chat support, and dedicated account managers for enterprise customers.

How quickly can I get started with Chatbot?

You can get started in minutes! Simply sign up, upload your knowledge base, and Chatot will be ready to assist your customers immediately.
